In 25 fall, I took an elective course on _Appreciation of English Short Stories_, which focused on the close reading of seven short stories by various authors such as _Hemingway, Faulkner, Mansfield_, and others. Each story will be studied over a two-week period, with a structured schedule consisting of one class for background introduction, two classes for close reading, and one class dedicated to group discussion.

As a mid-term assignment, we need to write a literary critique containing an analysis of the story's elements. I chose the short story _The use of Force_. [Here](../files/Literary criticism for The Use of Force.pdf) is my review for it.

For final assignment, students were required to write a literary critique that incorporates some literary theory. I analyzed the novel _The £1,000,000 Bank-Note_ and you can find it [here](../files/Literary criticism for The £1,000,000 Bank-Note.pdf).
